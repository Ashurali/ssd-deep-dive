4.3.2 Write Amplification — know this cold

IBM Plex Sans 400 · 15px/1.6 — body

Because GC does extra internal writes, the SSD writes more to flash than the host sent. That ratio is write amplification (寫放大) — and the whole design goal is keeping it low.
