4.3.1 GC principle, via a toy SSD · p. 16–26 · Figs 4-14→4-22

The book builds a tiny fictional SSD to make this concrete — follow it, because the intuition transfers exactly to real drives: 4 channels, 1 die each, 6 blocks per die, 9 squares per block. 20 blocks = user capacity; 4 blocks = OP.

Sequential writes stripe across the 4 dies Fig 4-15 — data goes to different channels in parallel. Keep writing until the user space is full — but the flash isn't full yet, because OP exists…
