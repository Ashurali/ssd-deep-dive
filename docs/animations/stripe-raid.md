# Stripe RAID & the Chained Warships

Four data dies plus an XOR parity die. Kill a die's block and rebuild it bit by bit from the survivors — the XOR runs for real. Then try to garbage-collect just one member of a stripe and watch the chain yank: the parity equation forces the whole stripe to move together, warships chained at Red Cliffs.
